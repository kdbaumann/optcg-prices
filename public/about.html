What is Fulcrum Cards?

Fulcrum Cards is an independent price reference and valuation tool for the One Piece Trading Card Game (OPTCG) English edition. We track card prices, graded card values, booster box economics, and tournament prize card data across all main series and extra booster sets.

Our goal is to give collectors, players, and investors a single reliable place to understand the market — from common parallels to ultra-rare Red Super Alternate Arts.

We are not a store. We don't sell cards. We aggregate, analyze, and present pricing data from TCGPlayer, OPCardlist, eBay completed sales, Snkrdunk, and community sources.

What We Cover

Every set from OP-01 Romance Dawn through OP-15 and all Extra Boosters (EB-01 through EB-04), Premium Boosters (PRB-01, PRB-02), and promo/tournament cards. We track raw NM prices, PSA 10 and BGS graded values, Japanese vs. English market comparisons, grading arbitrage opportunities, and sealed product box/case values.

Data Sources

Prices are sourced from: OPCardlist (median and last sold), TCGPlayer (market and direct), eBay completed sales, Snkrdunk (Japan), PriceCharting, and PSA / BGS pop report data. All prices are for reference only and may not reflect real-time market conditions.
