⚓ Regionals — Participation & Event Packs

Regional Participation Packs are given to all players who attend a qualifying Offline Regional. Event Packs are top-finisher prizes at Regionals and Flagship Battle events (typically Top 8 / Top 4 finishers). Note: the CS 25–26 Event Pack listed under Worlds contains championship-stamped versions of the same cards.

☠ Championship Finals — Regionals & Worlds

Championship packs are the most exclusive cards in the game — distributed only to top-placing players at the largest events. Top Player Packs go to top-ranked finalists. Celebration Packs contain championship-stamped versions of recent Store Tournament Pack cards. Event Packs (CS) are championship-stamped versions of the Flagship/Regional Event Packs. These are the cards that command $300–$2,000+ on the secondary market.

🇯🇵 Japanese Flagship Battle & Standard Battle Promos

The Flagship Battle is Japan's premier in-store competitive event, held periodically at card shops nationwide. 🇨🇳 Chinese & Asian Exclusive Promos

Bandai produces Simplified Chinese (Mainland China) and Traditional Chinese (Taiwan/HK) versions of the game, with region-exclusive promos from conventions, store events, and cultural celebrations. These cards use different card numbering and artwork specific to the Chinese market. Very limited supply outside China makes them highly sought-after internationally. Note: "For Asia" Japanese promos (like Cavendish EB01-012 and Perona OP06-093 above) are separate from these China-specific cards.
